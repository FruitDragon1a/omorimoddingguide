OMORI MODDING GUIDE

Welcome!!

Currently, we've been working on modding OMORI. But we've noticed something— and that's that there's a lack of tutorials teaching one about RPG Maker MV through the lens of OMORI modding.

So together we, FruitDragon & TomatoRadio, have compiled this guide for nearly all aspects of creating an OMORI mod!

Click on one of the below table of contents to take you to the section about it! They are designed to be read from top to bottom, with repeating concepts directing you back to what you learned above (don't worry; there are hyperlinks to relevant sections!) For navigation when within a section, please use the Table of Contents sidebar.
